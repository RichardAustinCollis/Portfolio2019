    About Me


    Born in Elwood, Indiana, I started my passion for tech after ripping apart my dad's broken computer, removing the parts that were apparently broken, and fixing it, all at the age of 8. At 12 I started my first dive into code on a game called 'Starcraft' which provided players with the ability to change code in order to make different levels. My brother introduced me to the idea, but the internet provided me with more information on how I could achieve my ideas. Of course, over the years, computers, electronics, programs, games, and other devices have improved and become more intricate, the task would be more difficult for 8 and 12-year-old Austin to accomplish today. I've always loved technology and would love to make some games or robotics and thats what drives me to complete this course. Life is messy and tends to take us in directions we didn't intend. Finally, I'm working towards a goal I can be proud of and become someone I've always wanted to be. Old jobs have given me skills, like I can drive like a 'bat-outta-hell' from my 'Jimmy John's' days, how to do basic medical procedors from my days learning from 'Eli Lilly', and from my job at 'Happy Dog' the correct way to train, handle, and care for dogs. Skills that could randomly be helpful in my life, but less helpful when it comes to coding.
